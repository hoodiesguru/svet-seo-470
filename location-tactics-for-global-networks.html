Location Tactics for Global Networks

Publikoval Pavel Procházka | 08. 07. 2026 | ⏰ 1 min čtení

Regional relevance is critical. When deploying a network of sites, attaching precise geographic tags (such as ICBM coordinates and Dublin Core schemas) helps search engines understand the resource.

By rotating local business schemas across sites, entities can mimic organic geographic distribution without maintaining physical servers in those areas.
